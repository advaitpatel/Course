  • Files: AsyncJokeServer.java, AsyncJokeClient.java, AsyncJokeAdminClient.java, AsyncJokeLog.txt, Checklist-AsyncJoke.html
  • Concatenate together representative console output in AsyncJokeLog.txt
  • Complete your regular JokeServer assignment.
  • Request jokes/proverbs as usual.

Server

  • In the worker thread, once a request has been received, break the TCP/IP connection.
  • Sleep for 40 seconds.
  • Using UDP, connect to back to (call back to) your client to send back the results.

Client

  • Because this is an async design, the client must maintain a (UDP) server. Listen for a callback from the server at an available UDP port, which must be sent to any server communicating with this client.
  • After you send the request for Joke/Proverb have your client display a prompt for adding strings of numbers together and displaying the results on the console. Keep displaying the prompt to get more numbers in a loop, until the joke/proverb is ready to display. Wait until after the latest cycle of getting numbers from the console, an displaying the results, before displaying a joke/proverb: 
    Press Return for joke or proverb:
Enter numbers to sum: 3 66 8 12
Your sum is: 89
Enter numbers to sum: 9 50
Your sum is: 59
A stich in time saves nine
Press Return for joke or proverb:
Note: It is possible that you will not have to modify your JokeServer Admin client.
